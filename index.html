Who We Are

Yanboua Al-Khair is a non-profit civil organization that operates according to the principles of humanity, neutrality, and independence, aiming to serve people without discrimination through humanitarian and development programs across all Iraqi governorates.

Vision, Mission & Goals

Vision

To be a leading humanitarian and development organization in Iraq, contributing effectively to improving quality of life and promoting a culture of sustainable development.

Mission

To provide effective humanitarian assistance and development programs targeting vulnerable groups and low-income families, with a focus on empowering people and building their capacities.

Goals

  • Provide humanitarian and relief assistance to the most vulnerable families.
  • Support and fund small and micro income-generating projects.
  • Empower youth and women through training and capacity building.
  • Raise awareness on sustainable development and climate change.
  • Promote a culture of volunteering and social responsibility.

Our Programs

Humanitarian Assistance & Relief

Food baskets, limited medical support, and emergency response for vulnerable and low-income families.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Supporting small projects, vocational training, and community-based productive initiatives.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Training workshops on life skills, leadership, and entrepreneurship, and encouraging youth-led initiatives.

Who We Are

Yanboua Al-Khair is a non-profit civil organization that operates according to the principles of humanity, neutrality, and independence, aiming to serve people without discrimination through humanitarian and development programs across all Iraqi governorates.

Vision, Mission & Goals

Vision

To be a leading humanitarian and development organization in Iraq, contributing effectively to improving quality of life and promoting a culture of sustainable development.

Mission

To provide effective humanitarian assistance and development programs targeting vulnerable groups and low-income families, with a focus on empowering people and building their capacities.

Goals

  • Provide humanitarian and relief assistance to the most vulnerable families.
  • Support and fund small and micro income-generating projects.
  • Empower youth and women through training and capacity building.
  • Raise awareness on sustainable development and climate change.
  • Promote a culture of volunteering and social responsibility.

Our Programs

Humanitarian Assistance & Relief

Food baskets, limited medical support, and emergency response for vulnerable and low-income families.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Supporting small projects, vocational training, and community-based productive initiatives.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Training workshops on life skills, leadership, and entrepreneurship, and encouraging youth-led initiatives.
