A Spring of Goodness for All Iraqi Cities

Yanboua Al-Khair Organization for Humanitarian Aid and Sustainable Development works to serve people, support vulnerable groups, and implement development projects that contribute to a decent life and a better future across all Iraqi cities and governorates.

Learn About Us Join Our Volunteers

Who We Are

Yanboua Al-Khair Organization is a non-profit civil organization that operates according to the principles of humanity, neutrality, and independence. The organization aims to serve people without discrimination based on gender, religion, ethnicity, or affiliation.

The organization supports vulnerable groups, empowers youth and women, and implements development programs that enhance social stability and help build resilient communities across all Iraqi cities and governorates.

Vision, Mission & Goals

Vision

To be a leading humanitarian and development organization in Iraq, actively contributing to improving quality of life and promoting a culture of sustainable development across all Iraqi cities.

Mission

To provide effective humanitarian assistance and implement development programs targeting vulnerable groups and low-income families, with a focus on empowering people, building their capacities, and strengthening their resilience to economic, social, and environmental challenges.

Goals

  • Provide humanitarian and relief assistance to the most vulnerable families.
  • Support and fund small and micro projects for sustainable livelihoods.
  • Empower youth and women through training and capacity building.
  • Raise community awareness on sustainable development and climate change.
  • Promote a culture of volunteering and social responsibility across Iraq.

Our Programs & Fields of Work

Humanitarian Assistance & Relief Program

Targets vulnerable and low-income families by providing food baskets, limited health and medical support, and rapid response in emergencies.

Sustainable Development & Livelihoods Program

Focuses on supporting small and micro income-generating projects, providing vocational and professional training, and encouraging productive community initiatives.

Youth & Women Empowerment Program

Organizes training workshops in life skills, leadership, and entrepreneurship, and supports youth-led volunteer and community initiatives, while enhancing women's participation in community and economic life.
